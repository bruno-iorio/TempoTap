Welcome!

The goal of the game is to press notes as they come down from the right side of the screen.

Place your fingers on the F, G, J, K keys, and tap them when the moving notes cross the line next to the keys.

Longer notes require you to hold the key to gain more points.

Hitting multiple consecutive notes starts a combo streak, which further increases the amount of points gained!

Have fun!
